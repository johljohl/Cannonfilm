About

Cannon Films Inc. was an American film production-distribution company active between 1979 and 1994, most known for its action, adventure, sci-fi and horror films. It was founded in 1979 by Menahem Golan and his cousin Yoram Globus. At its peak in 1986, Cannon Films produced and/or distributed over sixty films a year, making it one of the largest independent movie companies ever. In total they produced or released over 250 films. After years of unsuccessful attempts to launch their own studios (Golan's in Israel and Globus' in London), the cousins set up offices in New York City and formed "Globus International" as a base for their activities.
